Can I offer you an egg in this trying time?

Ingredients for this recipe

  1. Eggs
  2. Water
  3. Green dye

How to prepare this recipe:

  1. Place eggs in the bottom of a saucepan. Be sure not to crowd the eggs in the pan. They should fit comfortably.
  2. Fill the pan with cold water, 1 inch above the eggs.
  3. Bring the water to a rapid boil on the stovetop over high heat.
  4. Add green egg dye
  5. Once the water comes to a boil, cover the pan with a lid and remove the pan from the heat. Do not lift the lid. Set a timer for the type of boiled egg you want, from 4 minutes to 12 minutes.
  6. Fill a large bowl with ice and water.
  7. When the eggs reach the desired cooking time, use tongs to remove the eggs from the hot water and immerse gently into the prepared ice water to cool, about 10 minutes.
